AGENT: Dark Orchid 
by KC Esber 


Rules: 

1. Don't ever question the mission that is given to you. 
2. Agents should never ever feel guilty. 
3. Never ever reveal your true identity to people who cannot be trusted. 
4. Don't mix your personal life with your agent life. 
5. Never ever fall in love. 
I really didn't expect that my life would just end this way. I didn't know that my death would be this slow and 
painful. Who does? I just didn't expected it to be this spontaneous. I'm used to killing people especially the 
bad ones but I didn't expect that when it's already my turn to die it would be like this. 

I can say I lived a good life. Having both good and evil people around. Just the right balance. I experienced how 
life is amazing but at the same time rough and complicated. I lived two different lives. Best of both worlds as 
they say. A normal person and an agent. Risking my life for Cassiopeia Agency. Killing evil people but the same 
time being a sister, dancer and a fashion designer. 

I thought everything was normal but when I met him everything changed. The guy that made me break the 5th 
rule. That guy that made happy and suffer at the same time. Him. 

I'm Agent Dark Orchid and this is my story.
